Troubleshooting FAQ

Why didn't I receive a purchased package?

Oh no! You're supposed to receive it as soon as you log in!
If you don't receive it immediately, wait a bit! Our plugin could just need a little catch up!
Otherwise, you can reach out to us via our Contact page and we'll get back to you ASAP!

I have evidence of a player breaking the rules!

Yay! Thank you for helping us keep the server safe!
Please let one of the mods know in Discord by tagging them in a Support channel OR post it on our Forums! We'll see it and investigate!

I found a bug!

Wow! You bug hunter, you! You can submit a bug report on our Forums for the backend team to see and patch!

A user is harassing/threatening me :(

Well that's not very nice of them >:(
We take harassment very seriously. All content must abide by Mojang's rules, our terms, and (if Discord) Discord's Terms.
All of our rules are on our forums. Please follow those, including all harassment rules.
If you are being threatened by someone, please let a staff member know immediately.
If you feel like there is a real threat, please contact your local authorities.

The Discord server is under lockdown?

You will mainly only be greeted with the "under-lockdown" channel if there is a raid going on.
In the event of a raid, our team is prepared to alert the team to the raid and immediately start cleaning up. Rest assured we'll be back online soon!
If you need suport during a raid, see our Contact page for further support.

Are your servers down???????

They might be! Check our Status pages to see if our fleet is offline!

Give me free stuff!

No.

How can I join the PC Team?

Glad you asked! You can fill out our form here, or reach out to us via our Contact form to apply for another position!
