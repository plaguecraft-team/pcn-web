PlagueCraft Network Privacy Policy

We are Team Moonqii ("we", "our", "us"). We're dedicated to making sure your info is safe with us.
What information we contain about you
The types of data we hold and collect includes:
Your name/Minecraft username (dependent on service)
Your Email address
Your IP Address
Further data may be collected, perhaps if you fill out additional fields on our forums.
We collect some/all of this information in the following cases:
You register as a member of our forums.
You fill out our contact form.
You browse this site.
You fill out fields on your profile.
How your info is used
We may use your information in the following ways:
For the purposes of making you a registered member of our site, in order for you to contribute content to the Forums.
We may use your email to inform you of activity on our website.
Your IP is recorded on our server when you make a connection/specific action to/on our site and/or Minecraft server. Your IP will never be publicly visible.
Rights
You have a right to access any personal information we collect about you. If you would like to collect that information, please contact our privacy team at plaguecraftnetwork@gmail.com.
You also have the right to request your info and account to be erased. If you would like to do so, please contact plaguecraftnetwork@gmail.com.
Acceptance
Continued use of this site signifies you accept this policy. If you do not accept this policy, please cease the use of this site(s). When registering, we will further request your explicit acceptance of the privacy policy.
Changes to this policy
We may make changes to this policy at any time. At any point, you may be asked again whether you agree to this privacy policy or not if/when it changes in the future.

EFFECTIVE: Oct. 25, 2020
For privacy concerns contact plaguecraftnetwork@gmail.com
